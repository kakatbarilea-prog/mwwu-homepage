How It Works

From selecting your grapes to enjoying your first celebratory pour, our expert staff guides you through each step-making the winemaking experience seamless, social, and truly unforgettable.

Select

Select your varietal with guidance from our instructors-choose from bold reds, crisp whites, and seasonal favorites, all sourced from renowned wine regions.

Crush

Experience the crush and destemming process with modern hydraulic presses, then transfer the freshly pressed juice into barrel for aging.

Wait

Witness the transformation as your wine is racked, aged, and refined. You are welcome to be part of the process, helping to shape its final profile

Sip

Bottle and label your wine to take home, ready to share with friends, complement a meal, and celebrate your own creation.
